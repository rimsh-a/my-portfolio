01 — About

Small components, built properly.

I'm Rimsha Ijaz, a frontend developer who likes proving ideas by shipping them. Instead of only reading about how a calculator or an audio player works, I build one — keypad logic, playback state, image transitions, all of it wired by hand in vanilla HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

I care about layouts that hold together on any screen, interactions that feel immediate, and code a teammate could read without a walkthrough. This site is itself a project: semantic markup, a responsive grid, and motion that's there to guide — not distract.
